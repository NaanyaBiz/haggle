# Haggle

Pulls smart-meter usage from AGL Australia's *Neighbourhood* customer
portal and feeds it into the Home Assistant Energy dashboard.

## Setup

1. After installing via HACS, restart Home Assistant.
2. *Settings* -> *Devices & Services* -> *Add integration* -> **AGL Haggle**.
3. Enter your AGL email and password. AGL will SMS / email you a
   one-time code; enter it on the next step.
4. The integration polls daily. AGL data is delayed 24-48 h, so don't
   expect real-time numbers.

## Sensors

- **Grid import** (`sensor.agl_*_grid_import`) — cumulative kWh, fits the
  Energy dashboard *Grid consumption* slot.
- **Grid export** (`sensor.agl_*_grid_export`) — cumulative kWh, fits the
  *Return to grid* slot.
